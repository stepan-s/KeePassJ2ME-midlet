KeePass for J2ME
nao@phoneid.org

* This package requires JDK to be installed.  java.exe and jar.exe must be in the path.

- unzip KeePassJ2ME-*.zip
- Run KeePassInstaller.bat
- Choose your KeePass Key Database File, Database.kdb (or in a different name)
- This will create KeePassJ2ME-KDB.jar.  Download it to your J2ME phone through USB, Bluetooth, etc.
- On your J2ME phone, launch KeePass for J2ME.
