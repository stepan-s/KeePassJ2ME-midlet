About PhoneID.org

PhoneID.org was founded by Naomaru Itoi, and is run by him and several volunteers.

PhoneID.org develops secure, open-source, easy-to-use user authentication solutions.
PhoneID.org provides software development, support, product enhancement, and product integration services.    

Please contact us for requesting such service.
